About InterFlix

Movies are more than entertainment

Movies come to life through the imagination of viewers and that is what we are; film fanatics just like you. InterFilx is the largest growing movie library in our corner of the web.

Our Values

MISSION: Bringing movie inspiration to all who wonder and seek to one day grace the silver screen.

VISION: Our vision is to see no film fan scrolling through the deep wealth of movie options without direction. InterFlix is there to help YOU to be the best viewer you can be.
